# Curated company logos

These same-origin assets identify companies in the local InternJobs interface. A logo is configured once on the company record; individual jobs do not carry duplicate assets.

- `cloudflare.ico` — Cloudflare favicon downloaded from `https://www.cloudflare.com/favicon.ico` on 2026-07-22. Cloudflare also publishes its marks in its official press kit.
- `figma.svg` — unmodified full-color icon from Figma's official downloadable brand pack. Use is subject to Figma's published trademark guidelines.
- `databricks.svg` — unmodified `Databricks Symbol/databricks-symbol-color.svg` from Databricks' official Brandfolder logo pack, retrieved 2026-07-22. The Databricks brand guide prefers the full-color mark and prohibits recoloring.
